final boolean com.mesaverde.groups.Constants.PG_OUTCOME_EPIGENETIC_EFFECT = true
static

PG Game Epigenetic effect? When this is true, agent offspring choose their hierarchical preference based on their parents experience in the PG game in the last year. If the parent household's benefits from the game outweighed their costs, they adopt their parent's preference. If the costs outweighed the benefits, they adopt the opposite preference.
